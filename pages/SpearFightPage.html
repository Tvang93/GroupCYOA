Practice makes perfect.

It is finally time for your battle with the hag. You and your mentor find the Hag in a swamp. You ready your spear, confident in all your training thus far. You notice the hag raising her finger and begin chanting some incantations. You charge desperately at the hag and thrusting for her head. Your thrust aims true and pierces through her neck preventing her from finishing the spell. With another flick of your wrist, you slice her head clean off.
